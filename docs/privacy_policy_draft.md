# Klar — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 20, 2026**

*This Privacy Policy is currently under review by legal counsel and may 
be updated. Operated by Lucas Barrios.*

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data Klar collects, why, 
and how it's protected, in plain language. For the legal terms 
governing your use of Klar generally, see our [Terms of 
Service](/terms).

---

## 1. What Data We Collect

When you complete the Germany Readiness Diagnostic, we collect: your 
name, email, country, age, German and English level, education level, 
field of study, work experience, your timeline, your financial 
situation, your current location, and any additional context you 
choose to share.

If you purchase the Germany Application Kit and generate documents, we 
additionally process whatever details you choose to add — for example, 
an employer name, address, or phone number you enter to complete your 
CV.

We never ask for more than what's needed to assess your readiness or 
generate your documents.

---

## 2. Why We Collect It, and Our Legal Basis

We process your data to deliver the service you've asked for — 
generating your Diagnostic, matching you to real positions, and 
drafting your application documents. Under GDPR, this is processed on 
the basis of **performance of a contract** (Article 6(1)(b)): you've 
asked us to provide this assessment, and we need this information to 
do it.

Where you've explicitly checked a consent box on the intake form, we 
also rely on your **explicit consent** (Article 6(1)(a)) for that 
specific processing.

We keep an internal audit record of key actions (such as a diagnostic 
being approved) as a **legal obligation** under the EU AI Act's 
transparency and record-keeping requirements (Article 6(1)(c)).

---

## 3. Human Review — Not a Fully Automated Decision

Your Diagnostic score is generated by AI, but it is **always reviewed 
by a human consultant before it reaches you**. This is not a solely 
automated decision under GDPR Article 22 — a person reviews and 
approves every result.

---

## 4. Who We Share Your Data With

We use a small number of carefully chosen service providers to operate 
Klar. None of them are permitted to use your data for their own 
purposes.

| Service | What it does | Where | Data shared |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Anthropic** | Generates your Diagnostic score and documents | United States | Your profile information, sent for processing only — not stored by Anthropic |
| **Supabase** | Stores your data | EU (Frankfurt, Germany) | All data you provide |
| **Stripe** | Processes payment for the Application Kit | United States/Ireland | Payment details only — Klar never sees or stores your card number |
| **Resend** | Sends you transactional emails (approval notice, payment confirmation) | EU (Ireland) | Your name and email address only |
| **LangSmith** | Helps us monitor and improve the AI's performance | United States (routed via an EU endpoint) | A version of your request with personal details (name, financial information, address, phone) automatically removed before it's logged |

Where data leaves the EU (Anthropic, Stripe, LangSmith), this is 
covered by Standard Contractual Clauses, the standard legal mechanism 
for protecting data transferred outside the EU.

---

## 5. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your data for 24 months from your last activity with Klar. 
You can request earlier deletion at any time — see Section 6.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data ("right to erasure")
- **Receive a copy** of your data in a portable format
- **Object** to certain processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@kairosconsulting.co. 
We will respond within the timeframe required by GDPR.

---

## 7. Security

Your data is protected in transit via HTTPS, stored in an EU-based 
database with row-level access controls, and accessible only to 
authorized systems and our reviewing consultant.
