## Project Summary

Klar is a full-stack AI-powered Germany readiness diagnostic platform for
Spanish-speaking Latin Americans pursuing university, Ausbildung, or work
visa pathways in Germany. Students complete an 11-question conversational
intake form and receive a personalised readiness score (0–100), a
month-by-month roadmap, and three specific recommendations. Every result
is reviewed by a human consultant before delivery — satisfying EU AI Act
Article 14 and GDPR Article 22.
